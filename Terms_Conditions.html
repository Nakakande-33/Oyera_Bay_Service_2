Oyera Auto Service Bay

System Guidelines & Operating Terms

Terms and Conditions of Service

Welcome to the Oyera Auto Service Bay Ltd (OAS Bay) Records Management Portal. By utilizing this system to log vehicle entries, assign technicians, and process service bills, users agree to adhere to the standard operating business rules outlined below:

1. Service Scope & Vehicle Classification

  • OAS Bay provides specialized servicing for heavy, small, and commercial motor vehicles[cite: 1].
  • Approved minor repairs and routine servicing include engine oil and filter changes, gearbox oil and filter changes, brake fluid flushes, brake pad replacements, greasing, wheel alignment, and wheel balancing[cite: 1].
  • A single vehicle may be booked for one or multiple independent services within the same operating day[cite: 1].

2. Personnel & Labor Allocation

  • Vehicle servicing workflows are flexible; a single scheduled car service can be managed by one technician or distributed among multiple technicians depending on complexity[cite: 1].
  • All mechanical assessments must be validated by a designated senior technician, who holds sole authority to determine the specific replacement parts and fluids required for the vehicle prior to service execution[cite: 1].

3. Financial Regulations & Pricing Structure

  • Currency Standard: All financial records, receipts, and monetary assessments handled within this system must be calculated and displayed strictly in Ugandan Shillings (UGX)[cite: 1].
  • Base Labor Charge: A flat standard fee of UGX 20,000 is automatically applied for standard labor costs once a vehicle is officially placed into a service bay[cite: 1].
  • Wheel Clinic Rates: Wheel alignment services carry a fixed charge of UGX 30,000, and wheel balancing services carry a fixed charge of UGX 20,000[cite: 1].
  • Parts Inventory: Repair parts and fluids are sold directly on-site. System records must reflect actual itemized costs based on market variance ranges (Engine oils: UGX 79,000 - 200,000; Brake fluids: UGX 13,000 - 20,000; Oil filters: UGX 15,000 - 20,000)[cite: 1].

4. Data Integrity & System Responsibility

  • As part of the Refactory software engineering records management framework, users are strictly responsible for accurately logging vehicle number plates, tracking technician assignments, and updating service queue statuses ("In Bay" or "Serviced") in real time[cite: 1, 2].
